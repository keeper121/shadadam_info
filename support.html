Common Questions

Do I need an account?

No. Shadadam is designed for local party play and does not require an account.

Can I play offline?

Core gameplay works locally on your device.

How do tilt controls work?

During a round, tilt the phone forward to mark a card correct and tilt it back to skip. You can also use the on-screen buttons.
