Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Review this policy again after adding any new SDKs, analytics, accounts, purchases, or server features.

Overview

Shadadam Party Game is designed for local group play. The app does not require users to create an account, and it does not ask users to enter their name, email address, phone number, or password.

Information Stored On Your Device

Shadadam may store gameplay preferences and local app data on your device, such as selected language, selected game mode, selected card packs, team setup, hidden cards, settings, and local play statistics. This information is used to make the app work and to remember your preferences.

Advertising

Shadadam may show ads using Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect and process device information, advertising identifiers, app activity, approximate location, diagnostics, and other data as described in Google's policies. This information may be used to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at Google's partner sites policy.

Purchases

If paid features or subscriptions are offered, purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. Shadadam does not receive your full payment card details.

Children

Shadadam is a general-audience party game. The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review the issue.

Third-Party Services

Shadadam may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for app distribution and purchases

Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you change it, reset it, or uninstall the app. Data handled by third-party services is retained according to those services' policies.

Your Choices

You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device. You can control advertising tracking and personalization through your device settings where available.
